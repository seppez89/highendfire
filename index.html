What is a Blue Bottom Wave 1 One Piece Romance Dawn box?

The Blue Bottom Wave 1 Romance Dawn booster box is the very first print run of the One Piece Card Game's debut set (OP-01). Identified by the blue colour on the bottom of the box, these are extremely rare and considered the most valuable sealed product in the One Piece TCG. Since release, prices have surged over 300% and continue to climb as sealed supply dwindles. It's widely regarded as a blue-chip TCG investment.

What is the Dragon Ball Fusion World Energy Marker E-81 Gold Parallel?

The Energy Marker E-81 Gold Parallel is an ultra-rare chase card from the Dragon Ball Fusion World Manga Booster. It features Cell and Super Saiyan Gohan with a stunning gold foil parallel treatment. This is a Japanese exclusive and one of the hardest pulls in the Dragon Ball Super Card Game. Gold parallels from this set command premium prices due to extremely low pull rates.

How much is a complete 1st Edition Fossil Set worth?

A complete 1st Edition Fossil Set with all 62 cards in Near Mint condition is currently valued at approximately $10,000 AUD. This is one of the rarest complete WOTC Pokemon sets available and a blue-chip investment collectible from 1999. Prices have appreciated significantly year over year, with NM-Mint complete sets becoming increasingly scarce as more are broken up for individual grading.
